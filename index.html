About WBC Analysis

White Blood Cell (WBC) differential count is a crucial diagnostic test that determines the percentage of different types of white blood cells in your blood. Our AI-powered system classifies five main types:

  • Neutrophils – Fight infections
  • Lymphocytes – Immune response
  • Monocytes – Remove dead cells
  • Eosinophils – Combat parasites
  • Basophils – Allergic responses
